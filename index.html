Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about FlowDesk.

Is FlowDesk free to use?

Yes! FlowDesk has a generous free tier for freelancers and small teams. You can manage up to 50 leads and 10 active clients with no credit card required.

Do I need technical skills to get started?

Not at all. FlowDesk is designed to be intuitive from day one. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can use FlowDesk — no training or setup sessions needed.

Can I import my existing contacts?

Absolutely. FlowDesk supports CSV imports so you can bring in contacts from spreadsheets or other tools in just a few minutes.
